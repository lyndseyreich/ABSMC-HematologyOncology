Before you start your Hematology/Oncology rotation...

Please answer the following questions. Your responses will help us understand your baseline confidence and guide improvements to the rotation.

1. I feel confident interpreting common hematology lab abnormalities (e.g., CBC, peripheral smear).

2. I understand the initial evaluation and risk stratification of common oncologic emergencies (e.g., neutropenic fever).

3. I feel prepared to manage common inpatient hematology/oncology consults.

4. I can identify patients who require urgent hematology/oncology evaluation.

5. I am comfortable counseling patients and families about new hematology/oncology diagnoses.

6. I can create an initial diagnostic and management plan for common hematology conditions.

7. I can create an initial diagnostic and management plan for common oncology conditions.

8. I am familiar with the indications and basic interpretation of flow cytometry, cytogenetics, and molecular testing.

9. I can identify patients who may benefit from palliative care involvement during hospitalization.

10. Overall, I feel ready to begin my Hematology/Oncology rotation.
