Pre-Survey

Please complete this short survey before beginning your Hematology/Oncology rotation.




1. I feel confident in my ability to manage common hematology/oncology problems.

2. I understand the basic pathophysiology of common hematologic malignancies.

3. I am comfortable interpreting basic hematology lab results (CBC, coagulation panel, etc.).

4. I am familiar with initial management of oncologic emergencies (e.g., tumor lysis, spinal cord compression).

5. I feel prepared to participate in goals-of-care discussions with patients who have cancer.

6. I can identify appropriate situations for referral to hematology/oncology specialists.

7. I feel confident in evaluating chemotherapy-related toxicities.

8. I know where to find up-to-date, evidence-based guidelines for hematology/oncology care.
