Pre-Survey

Please answer the following questions honestly. Your responses will help us improve the rotation experience.

1. I feel confident in evaluating patients with hematologic malignancies.

2. I feel confident in evaluating patients with solid tumors.

3. I can confidently recognize and manage common oncologic emergencies.

4. I am comfortable discussing cancer diagnoses and prognoses with patients.

5. I am aware of the latest guidelines and literature relevant to oncology practice.

6. I feel well-prepared to manage complications of chemotherapy and immunotherapy.
