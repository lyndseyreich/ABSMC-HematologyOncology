Pre-Survey

Please answer the following questions honestly. Your responses will help us improve this resource.

1. I feel confident managing common hematology/oncology conditions.

2. I understand the basic workup for a patient with suspected malignancy.

3. I can interpret a complete blood count (CBC) with confidence.

4. I am comfortable initiating initial management for febrile neutropenia.

5. I understand when to involve hematology/oncology consultants.

6. I feel comfortable discussing prognosis and treatment options with patients and families.

7. I can recognize oncologic emergencies and act promptly.

8. I am familiar with the most common chemotherapy regimens used in inpatient settings.
